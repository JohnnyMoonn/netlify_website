The Cyrodill City Guard

The guards are led by a captain, who reports to the local Count or Countess.

Their duty is to uphold peace and order throughout the city and county, as well as ensuring justice is had in the event of a crime.

Additionally, they are the last line of defense of their city in the event of assault or invasion.

Every County in Cyrodiil has its own Guard, these guards enforce the law and protect the capital cities of each County and the other villages or settlements

All city guards wear very similar armor sets, differences are normally just the color of the sash and material of the undershirt used, either steel or chain mail.

City guards also carry a shield which varies in color, and typically displays the insignia of the County across the front.
